Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup water and 2 eggs well beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup flour, sifted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • About 1 cup oatmeal
  • Chopped nuts (walnuts preferred)

Directions

Mix in order and drop on baking sheet. Bake 400Â° 7 to 8 minutes. Then run under broiler to brown
