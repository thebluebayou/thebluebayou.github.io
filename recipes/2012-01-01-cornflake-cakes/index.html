Cornflake Cakes

Ingredients

  • 200g/8oz chocolate
  • 2tbsp/30ml golden syrup
  • 50g/2oz unsalted butter
  • ornflakes

Method

  1. Melt the chocolate with the syrup and butter.
  2. Add enough cornflakes to mix.
  3. Spoon the mixture into paper cases and allow to set.
