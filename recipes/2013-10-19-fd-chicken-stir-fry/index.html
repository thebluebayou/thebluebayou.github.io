FD Chicken Stir Fry

281 calories

  • 140g chicken breast
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chopped ginger
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • Half a squeezed lemon

Cut chicken fillet cut into strips and fry all in non-stick pan, until browned and sealed

Add

  • 50g sugar snap peas
  • 100g finely sliced cabbage
  • 160g carrots, cut into thin strips

And cook for 5-10 more minutes until chicken is cooked, adding water if necessary
