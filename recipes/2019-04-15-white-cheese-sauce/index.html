White Cheese Sauce

Ingredients

250ml milk
25 grams butter
2 tablespoons flour
(50g cheese)

Method

Whisk fast as the butter melts and the mixture comes to the boil - the flour will disappear and the sauce will begin to thicken. Whisk for 2 minutes while the sauce bubbles and becomes nice & thick.

(Add cheese to make it cheese sauce)
