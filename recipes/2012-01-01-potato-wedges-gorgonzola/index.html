Potato Wedges and Gorgonzola Sauce - Nigel Slater

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg Floury Potatoes
  • 8 rashers of Smoked Streaky Bacon
  • 5 tbsp Groundnut or Rapeseed Oil
  • 1 tbsp crushed Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tbsp Smoked Paprika

for the Dressing

  • 250ml Double Cream
  • 150g Gorgonzola or Dolcelatte

(any blue cheese will work but milder & sweeter is better)

METHOD

Sweet Pepper & Cherry Tomatoes | Baked Mushroom Rice - Nigel Slater (4 people)
